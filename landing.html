How it works

Three steps to your
best Saturday ever.

No more bouncing between apps or scribbling addresses on scraps of paper. Fade & Find brings it all together.

01

Find everything nearby

Open the app, allow location, and instantly see estate sales, antique stores, auctions, flea markets, and thrift stores on one live map.

02

Build your route

Pin the spots that look promising. Fade & Find automatically optimizes your stops into the most efficient driving order — then hands it off to Google Maps.

03

Hunt smarter

Track where you've been, save favorites, flag closed businesses, and share your route with your crew. Every run gets better than the last.
